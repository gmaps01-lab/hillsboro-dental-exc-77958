Frequently Asked Questions About Invisalign Cost

How much does Invisalign treatment typically cost at Hillsboro Dental Excellence?
At Hillsboro Dental Excellence, the cost of Invisalign treatment usually ranges from $3,500 to $6,500, depending on the complexity of your case and how long your treatment will take. We offer complimentary consultations to assess your needs and provide a personalized quote. Flexible payment plans and financing options are available, and we can help you check if your dental insurance covers a portion of Invisalign costs.
How long does Invisalign treatment take from start to finish at your Hillsboro, OR office?
Most patients at our Hillsboro office complete their Invisalign treatment in 12 to 18 months, although minor cases can be finished in as little as 6 months. The exact timeline depends on your specific alignment needs and how diligently you wear your aligners (ideally 20-22 hours per day). During your initial consultation, our team will create a customized treatment plan and give you a more precise estimate for your case.
Is Hillsboro Dental Excellence licensed to provide Invisalign and sleep apnea treatments?
Yes, our doctors are fully licensed by the Oregon Board of Dentistry and have advanced certifications in both Invisalign and dental sleep medicine. Dr. Skinner is a certified Invisalign provider and has completed extensive training in sleep apnea dental treatments, ensuring you receive expert care at our Hillsboro practice.
What is the process for starting Invisalign treatment at your office?
The Invisalign process at Hillsboro Dental Excellence begins with a free consultation, where we assess your smile and discuss your goals. If you’re a good candidate, we use a digital iTero scanner to create 3D images of your teeth—no messy impressions needed. Our team then works with Invisalign to design your custom treatment plan. Once your aligners arrive (usually within a couple of weeks), we’ll schedule a fitting appointment and walk you through wearing and caring for your aligners. You'll come in for periodic checkups every 6 to 8 weeks to monitor progress.
Do you offer any guarantees or warranties for Invisalign results?
We stand behind our Invisalign treatments. While individual results vary, we offer a satisfaction commitment—if you wear your aligners as directed and are not satisfied with your results, we’ll work with you on additional refinement aligners at no extra charge for up to one year after your initial treatment is completed. We also provide ongoing retention guidance to help you maintain your new smile long-term.
